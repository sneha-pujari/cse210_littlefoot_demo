Littlefoot Demo

Special Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar[1]
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract[2]

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together butter and sugar until smooth.[3]
  3. Beat in egg and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually blend in flour.[4]
  5. Drop by spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  6. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes or until golden.[5]
  1. Secret: Add a pinch of brown sugar for extra depth.
  2. Grandma's tip: Use real vanilla beans for best flavor.
  3. Chef's secret: Add a tablespoon of honey at this stage.
  4. Pro tip: Chill the dough for 30 minutes before baking.
  5. Grandma's trick: Leave them slightly underbaked for chewier cookies.
